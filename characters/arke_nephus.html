Arke Nephus (Greek: Άρκε Νέφους, Arke Nefous, also referred to as “Arke the Fatalist”) is one of the main antagonists and is the ‘Order’ of the Scorched Hand. His ability is named Divine Retribution.

Appearance

Arke is of slightly above average height with a slim build. He’s quite pale and has bright blue eyes. He has long black hair which is arranged neatly in a bun. Dark red scars can be seen on his lower lip due to a habit of biting it, making it bleed.

Arke’s ability is named Divine Retribution (Greek: θεία Ανταπόδοση, Theia Antapodosi). At first, it is shown that his ability allows him to instantly kill someone through contact, either directly or indirectly.

It is later revealed to be a space-mind ability where the target’s consciousness is transported to a cathedral-themed mind space. Within the mind space, Arke commands the target to kneel before him before proceeding to give a statement regarding the target's guilt over ‘sins’ committed in their life. He then takes out a revolver and aims it at the target. The target, being unable to move and stuck in a kneeling position, gets shot. The bullet’s effectiveness is dependent on the level of guilt the target feels over their actions in the past. The damage is then carried over to the physical body of the target.
